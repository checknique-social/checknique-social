What is Checknique?

Checknique is a sofware solution that helps consumers verify the authenticity of products before purchase and keep track of their purchases. It uses a QR code or tag on the product, which consumers can scan with the app to determine if it's a genuine item or a counterfeit. The app also allows users to create a personal catalog of their purchases, share product information, and access product-related resources.
