CONSUMERS

Checknique helps consumers avoid counterfeit goods by letting them instantly verify a product’s authenticity using their phone. By scanning a unique QR code on the item, the Checknique app confirms if it's genuine and allows the buyer to register ownership, offering protection, peace of mind, and exclusive brand perks.

The Problem

Fakes are getting hard to detect because they look so real. Too real. “Consumers are unaware that they are buying a [fake] product because the counterfeit is just that good.” -Thomas Mahn, Port Director Louisville of the Custom and Border Patrol Agency.

  1. Buying fake products
  2. Waste money
  3. No ownership benefits
  4. Damage trust in brands

Products

Counterfeiters are targeting multiple product groups. By applying Checknique technology to your products we can engage the consumer, port authorities, and anyone along the supply chain to assist in stopping counterfeit goods from saturating the marketplace simply by scanning our codes and verifying authenticity of the product.

##ICONS GO HERE##

Scan

Use your smartphone to open the Checknique app and scan the QR code found on the product’s label or packaging. Each product has a unique digital signature, like a fingerprint, that connects to Checknique’s secure database.

Validate

In seconds, the app confirms whether the product is authentic or fake using Checknique’s dual-key QR system. This process is powered by a patent-pending technology that checks for duplicates, scan history, and geographic anomalies.

Register

Once verified, register the product in your name with one tap. This gives you digital proof of ownership and access to exclusive rewards, product updates, sales alerts, and more.

Benfit

Consumers are aware of your genuinely marked goods and will avoid purchasing a fake.

Consumer Engagement

Discounts and pushing notifications to notify them about your next big sale.

Consumer Driven

Their new confidence including your genuine products increases your brand credibility and market share.
